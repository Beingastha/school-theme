## 1. Getting started — logging in

1. Go to **https://excellencebhopal.in/wp-admin**
2. Enter your username and password.
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**. The dark menu down the left-hand side is where you go for everything.

> **Tip:** Keep your login details safe and don't share them. If you forget your password, click "Lost your password?" on the login screen.

**A note on words you'll see:**

| Word | What it actually means |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | The admin area — the "control room" of your website |
| **Publish / Update** | The blue Save button. Nothing goes live until you click it |
| **Media Library** | Your photo storage cupboard |
| **Featured Image** | The main photo for an item (shown on cards and at the top of its page) |
| **Widget** | A small block you can drag into the footer |

## 2. The golden rules

Four things that will save you a lot of confusion:

1. **Nothing saves until you click the blue button.** It's called **Save Changes**, **Publish**, or **Update** depending on the screen. If you leave the page without clicking it, your work is lost.

2. **Text is edited in one place, photos in another.** Words live in **Page Content**. Photos live in the item itself (**Featured Image**) or in **Customize**.

3. **If you don't see your change, it's almost always your browser showing an old copy.** Press **Ctrl + Shift + R** (Windows) or **Cmd + Shift + R** (Mac). See [section 15](#15-i-changed-it-but-i-cant-see-it).

4. **You can't permanently break anything by typing.** Changing text is always safe and always reversible. The only risky area is listed in [section 17](#17-what-not-to-touch).

## 3. Where do I change things? (the cheat sheet)

There are **three main places**. Almost everything lives in one of them.

| If you want to change… | Go to… |
|---|---|
| Wording on the Home, About, Academics or Admissions pages | **Page Content** (in the left menu) |
| Phone, email, address, logo, colours, principal's name, pass percentages | **Appearance → Customize → School Settings** |
| Facilities, Achievements, News, Circulars, Staff, Testimonials | Their own menu in the left sidebar |
| Events | **Events** in the left menu |
| The admission form fields | **Forminator → Forms** |
| Menus at the top of the site | **Appearance → Menus** |
| The footer at the bottom | **Appearance → Widgets** |
| Curriculum, Fee Structure, Staff and similar simple pages | **Pages** |

Don't worry about memorising this — each section below tells you exactly where to click.

## 4. Page Content — your main editing screen

This is where you'll spend most of your time. It controls **the words** on your four biggest pages.

**Go to: `Page Content` in the left menu** (look for the pencil-and-page icon).

Across the top you'll see five tabs:

- **🏠 Home** — the homepage
- **ℹ️ About** — the About Us page
- **📚 Academics** — the Academics page
- **📋 Admissions** — the Admissions page
- **📖 Content Guide** — a built-in reminder sheet

### How to edit anything here

1. Click the tab for the page you want (e.g. **📋 Admissions**).
2. Scroll to the box you want to change. Each box is clearly labelled — *Main Headline*, *Subtitle*, *Step Name*, *Question*, and so on.
3. Type your new text over the old.
4. Scroll to the bottom and click **Save Changes**.
5. Open the page on your website and refresh to see it.

### What's on each tab

**🏠 Home tab**
- Show/hide switches for every homepage section (see [section 7](#7-show-or-hide-whole-homepage-sections))
- The three big banner ("Hero") designs — headline and subtitle for each
- "Why Choose Us" heading + the 6 cards
- The Admissions banner in the middle of the page

**ℹ️ About tab**
- Page heading, "Our Story" paragraphs
- Vision & Mission text
- The 4 "Our Values" cards
- Principal's message quote and paragraph
- Bottom call-to-action heading

**📚 Academics tab**
- Page heading and intro
- Curriculum overview (Classes I–XII)
- Examination pattern
- The 3 "Our Approach" cards
- **Board Results** bar labels
- Academic facilities (3 items)

**📋 Admissions tab**
- Page subtitle
- The 4 "How to Apply" steps
- Eligibility table (4 rows)
- Documents required (up to 6 — *leave one blank to hide it*)
- Important dates (3)
- FAQs (up to 6 — *leave the question blank to hide that FAQ*)
- **Enquiry Form Section** — the intro text and the Admissions Office address/phone/email/hours box
- Bottom call-to-action heading

> **Handy:** Anywhere it says "leave blank to hide", clearing the box removes that item from the website. That's how you show only 4 documents instead of 6.

## 5. School Settings — phone, address, logo, colours

Anything that appears **all over the site** (like your phone number in the header and footer) is changed in one place, so you only ever type it once.

**Go to: `Appearance → Customize → School Settings`**

You'll see a live preview of your site on the right. As you type, the preview updates. **Click the blue `Publish` button at the top when you're done** — otherwise nothing saves.

Inside you'll find:

| Section | What's in it |
|---|---|
| **School Identity** | Full legal school name, short name, tagline, UDISE code |
| **Contact Information** | Phone, email, address, office hours, Google Maps embed |
| **Social Media Links** | Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube |
| **Key Statistics** | Pass rate, number of students, number of teachers, year established |
| **Principal's Message** | Principal's name, job title, quote and portrait photo |
| **Board Results** | The four bar-graph labels and their percentages (0–100) |
| **Student Life Mosaic** | The 6 photo tiles and their captions |
| **Appearance & Hero** | Which of the 3 banner designs to use, and the banner photo |
| **Colour Palette** | Pick a whole colour scheme for the site |
| **Font Pairing** | Pick the site's fonts |

### Changing your phone number (an example)

1. **Appearance → Customize → School Settings → Contact Information**
2. Change the phone number.
3. Click **Publish**.
4. It's now updated in the header, footer and contact page all at once.

### Changing the colours or fonts

**Colour Palette** and **Font Pairing** each offer several ready-made combinations designed to look good together. Pick one, watch the preview, and Publish. If you don't like it, pick the original again — nothing is lost.

> **Warning:** The Board Results percentages must be a number between **0 and 100**. Typing "99%" won't work — just type `99`.

## 6. Hindi translations — how the language button works

Your website has an **EN / हिं** button in the top corner. Visitors click it to switch the whole site to Hindi.

**This does not translate automatically.** Someone has to type the Hindi once. After that it's saved forever.

### How to add Hindi

In **Page Content**, under almost every English box there's a matching box with a small green **HINDI** tag. Type the Hindi translation there and click **Save Changes**.

That's it. When a visitor clicks हिं, your Hindi text appears instead of the English.

- If you **leave a Hindi box empty**, that bit stays in English. Nothing breaks.
- You can do this gradually — translate the most important pages first.

### Hindi in other places

| Where | How to add Hindi |
|---|---|
| Page Content boxes | The box with the green **HINDI** tag underneath |
| Menu links | **Appearance → Menus** → open a menu item → type Hindi in the **Description** field |
| Simple pages (Curriculum, etc.) | The **Hindi Translation** box below the main editor |
| Facilities / Achievements / News | Type Hindi directly in the title and description |

## 7. Show or hide whole homepage sections

Want to temporarily remove the Testimonials block from your homepage? You don't need anyone technical.

1. Go to **Page Content → 🏠 Home**
2. At the very top is **"Homepage Sections — Show / Hide"**
3. Flip the switch **off** for any section you want hidden.
4. Click **Save Changes**.

You can switch off any of these:

- Circulars & Notices
- Key Statistics Band
- Why Choose Us
- Principal's Message
- Academic Excellence (board results)
- Achievements
- Facilities
- Student Life Mosaic
- News & Events
- Testimonials
- Admissions CTA Banner
- Contact Section

> **Your content is not deleted.** Switching a section off just hides it. Switch it back on any time and everything returns exactly as it was.

## 8. Adding photos

### Uploading a photo

1. Go to **Media → Add New**, or click **Upload** wherever a photo is being chosen.
2. Drag your photo in, or click **Select Files**.

### Setting the main photo for an item

Most items (a Facility, an Achievement, a News post, the Principal's Desk page) use a **Featured Image**. This is the photo shown on its card and at the top of its own page.

1. Open the item you're editing.
2. On the **right-hand side**, find the **Featured Image** panel.
3. Click **Set featured image**, pick or upload your photo, click **Set featured image**.
4. Click **Update**.

> **Can't find the Featured Image panel?** It's in the right sidebar. If the sidebar is hidden, click the ⚙️ (gear) icon in the top-right corner to show it. Make sure the **Post** or **Page** tab is selected, not **Block**.

### Photo tips for a fast website

- **Resize before uploading.** Around **1600 pixels wide** is plenty. Photos straight off a phone or camera are far too large and will make your site slow.
- **Use JPG** for photographs.
- **Name files sensibly** — `science-lab.jpg`, not `IMG_20240817.jpg`.
- Aim to keep each photo **under 500 KB**.

## 9. The card sections (Facilities, Achievements, News…)

These sections show a **grid of cards**. Each card is its own entry that you add, edit or delete like a mini article.

Each of these has its **own menu in the left sidebar**:

| Menu | Where it appears | Has its own page? |
|---|---|---|
| **Facilities** | Homepage "Facilities" section | ✅ Yes — cards are clickable |
| **Achievements** | Homepage "Student Achievements" | ✅ Yes — cards are clickable |
| **News & Events** | Homepage "News & Events" | ✅ Yes |
| **Circulars & Notices** | Homepage notices strip | ✅ Yes |
| **Testimonials** | Homepage testimonials | ❌ Display only |
| **Why Choose Us** | Homepage "Why Choose Us" cards | ❌ Display only |
| **Staff** | Staff page | ❌ Display only |

### Adding a new one (e.g. a new Facility)

1. Click **Facilities** in the left menu.
2. Click **Add New**.
3. **Title** — the name (e.g. *Physics Laboratory*).
4. **Main box** — write the full description. This appears on its own page.
5. **Excerpt** — a one-line summary shown on the card. *(If you don't see an Excerpt box, click ⚙️ → Post → Excerpt.)*
6. **Featured Image** (right sidebar) — the photo.
7. Click **Publish**.

### Changing the order of cards

1. Open the item.
2. In the right sidebar find **Page Attributes → Order**.
3. Type a number: `1` shows first, `2` second, and so on.
4. Click **Update**.

### Deleting

Hover over the item in the list and click **Trash**. It goes to the bin and can be restored — it isn't gone forever.

> **Important — the "default cards" rule:** Some sections (Why Choose Us, Testimonials, Facilities, Achievements) come with sample cards built in so the site never looks empty. **The moment you publish your own first card, all the samples disappear** and only your real entries show. This surprises people — if your samples vanish after adding one item, that's normal and intended.

## 10. The Events Calendar

Your site uses a plugin called **The Events Calendar** for school events.

### Adding an event

1. Click **Events → Add New** in the left menu.
2. Enter the event name.
3. Fill in the **date and time** in the "Event Date & Time" section below the editor.
4. Optionally add a description, venue and photo.
5. Click **Publish**.

### Where your events appear

| Place | What shows |
|---|---|
| **https://excellencebhopal.in/events/** | The full month calendar |
| **Footer of every page** | A small month calendar — dates with events are highlighted in gold and clickable |

The footer calendar has **‹ ›** arrows to look at future months, and a **"View full calendar →"** link. It updates itself — you never edit the footer calendar directly. **Just add events and they appear automatically.**

### Changing calendar colours

**Appearance → Customize → The Events Calendar**

> **Careful here:** This is where the day numbers were once accidentally set to white, which made them invisible against the cream background. **Keep text colours dark.** If the calendar ever looks blank or unreadable, this setting is the first place to check.

## 11. The Admissions form

The enquiry form on the Admissions page is run by a plugin called **Forminator**.

### Changing the form's questions

1. Go to **Forminator → Forms**.
2. Hover over the admission form and click **Edit**.
3. Add, remove or reorder fields by dragging.
4. Click **Update**.

### Reading enquiries people have submitted

**Forminator → Submissions** — every enquiry sent through the form is stored here.

### Changing where enquiry emails go

1. Edit the form.
2. Go to the **Email Notifications** tab.
3. Change the recipient address.
4. **Update**.

> **Worth testing occasionally:** Submit a test enquiry yourself now and then to confirm the emails are still arriving (and check the spam folder). Email delivery can quietly stop working, and you'd rather find out yourself than miss a parent's enquiry.

## 12. Ordinary pages (Curriculum, Fee Structure…)

Simpler pages — Curriculum, Assessment Schedule, Fee Structure, Brief History, Scholarships, Student Cabinet and so on — are edited with WordPress's normal page editor.

1. Go to **Pages** in the left menu.
2. Hover over the page and click **Edit**.
3. Type directly into the page, like a Word document.
4. Click **Update**.

**Useful things you can add** by clicking the **+** button:
- Headings, paragraphs, bullet lists
- Tables (good for fee structures and timetables)
- Images and PDF/document links
- Buttons

### Adding Hindi to these pages

Scroll below the main editing area to the **Hindi Translation** box. Fill in the **Hindi Title** and **Hindi Content**, then **Update**. Leave it blank and the page simply stays in English.

### The Principal's Desk page

The **photo** on this page comes from the **Featured Image** panel on the right — not from inside the page text. There's a reminder note at the top of that screen.

## 13. Menus — the links at the top

**Go to: `Appearance → Menus`**

Your site has three menus:

- **Primary Menu** — the main links across the top
- **Mobile Drawer Menu** — the links inside the ☰ button on phones
- **Footer Quick Links** — links at the bottom

### Adding a link

1. Pick the menu you want from the dropdown at the top, click **Select**.
2. On the left, tick the page you want, click **Add to Menu**.
3. Drag it into position. **Drag it slightly to the right** to make it a sub-item (a dropdown).
4. Click **Save Menu**.

### Adding the Hindi version of a menu link

1. Click the small ▾ arrow on the menu item to open it.
2. Type the Hindi into the **Description** field.
3. **Save Menu**.

> **Can't see the Description field?** Click **Screen Options** at the very top-right of the page and tick **Description**.

## 14. The footer — the bottom of every page

The footer is built from **widgets** — small blocks you can add, remove and drag around.

**Go to: `Appearance → Widgets`**

You'll see five areas:

| Area | Currently holds |
|---|---|
| **Footer Column 1** | School logo, name and social media links |
| **Footer Column 2** | Quick Links list |
| **Footer Column 3** | Explore links list |
| **Footer Column 4** | The events mini calendar |
| **Footer Widgets** | A full-width strip below the four columns |

### Rearranging

**Drag any widget** from one column to another and it moves on the live site. Drag widgets within a column to reorder them.

### Useful widgets available to you

- **ESB: School Brand** — logo, school name, social links *(pulls automatically from School Settings)*
- **ESB: School Contact** — address, phone, email, UDISE *(pulls automatically from School Settings)*
- **ESB: Events Mini Calendar** — the month calendar of events
- **Custom HTML** — for a list of links
- **Text / Image** — anything else

> **Why some widgets have no settings:** *ESB: School Brand* and *ESB: School Contact* deliberately have no fields to fill in. They read your details live from **School Settings**, so your phone number can never end up different in the footer than everywhere else. To change them, edit **Customize → School Settings**.

If you empty a column completely, it falls back to its original built-in content — so you can't accidentally end up with a broken, empty footer.

## 15. "I changed it but I can't see it!"

This is the single most common worry, and it's almost never a real problem. Work down this list:

1. **Did you click the blue save button?** *Save Changes* / *Publish* / *Update*. This is the answer most of the time.

2. **Force your browser to fetch a fresh copy.** Your browser saves a copy of the site to load it faster, and often shows you that old copy.
   - Windows: **Ctrl + Shift + R**
   - Mac: **Cmd + Shift + R**

3. **Try a private/incognito window.** If your change shows there, it was definitely just your browser — the public is seeing the new version.

4. **Wait 2–3 minutes and refresh again.** The site keeps a short-term cache for speed.

5. **Check you edited the right place.** For example, the homepage banner text is under Page Content → Home → *the hero design currently in use*. If you edit "Hero 1" but the site is set to show "Hero 2", you won't see a change. The active one is set under *Customize → Appearance & Hero → Hero Layout*.

If it's still wrong after all five, note down exactly what you changed and where, and send that to your developer.

## 17. What NOT to touch

Everything in this guide is safe. These few things are not — please leave them to your developer:

| Avoid | Why |
|---|---|
| **Appearance → Theme File Editor** | This edits the website's actual code. One wrong character can take the whole site offline. |
| **Tools → Plugin/Theme File Editor** | Same reason. |
| **Deactivating or deleting plugins** | Removing *The Events Calendar* or *Forminator* would break your events and admission form. |
| **Switching to a different theme** | Your entire design would disappear. |
| **Deleting pages you didn't create** | Some pages are wired into menus and links. Deleting them causes "page not found" errors. |
| **Changing Settings → Permalinks** | This changes every web address on your site and breaks links other people have shared. |

**A safe habit:** if a screen is full of code, or you find yourself thinking *"I'm not sure what this does"* — stop and ask. It's much quicker to ask first than to repair afterwards.

### Before making big changes

Ask your host or developer about **backups**. Knowing you have a recent backup makes everything less stressful, and means a mistake is a five-minute fix rather than a crisis.
